# Strategic Product Leader

## About the Role

We are currently seeking a strategic Product Leader to drive innovation. In this role, you will operate at the intersection of business priorities, technical design, and AI enablement to translate complex opportunities into rapid prototypes and minimum viable products (MVPs).

---

## Who We Want: The Strategic Product Manager

We highly value non-traditional career paths. You are not a traditional backlog manager; you are a strategic leader who naturally bridges the gaps between engineering, design, and complex business needs.

### Key Traits

- **Relentlessly Curious**  
  You excel at advanced requirement discovery. You prefer diving into ambiguous problem spaces to uncover latent user needs through workshops and immersion, rather than waiting for perfectly written specs.

- **Outcome-Driven**  
  You practice extreme ownership over your product vision. You balance user needs with technical feasibility and measure your success strictly by business outcomes, not outputs.

- **AI-Empowered**  
  You champion AI literacy and lead the development of data-driven digital products using rapid prototyping and MVP-first methodologies.

- **Deep Business Immersion**  
  You possess the ability to rapidly learn the specialized language of highly complex industries so that you can think, speak, and strategize like an insider.

---

## What You Will Do

- Establish and manage lean product roadmaps focused on high-impact, minimal viable scope  
- Drive agile execution, leading ceremonies and ensuring short, data-driven feedback cycles  
- Manage complex stakeholder expectations across the organization, clearly communicating trade-offs and MVP outcomes from the frontline to the C-suite  
- Collaborate cross-functionally to test hypotheses, champion A/B testing, and refine solutions based on real user feedback  

---

## Ideal Candidate

If you are a resourceful problem solver who thrives in ambiguity and knows how to turn big ideas into measurable enterprise value, we want to talk to you.

- Ability to work in an onsite setting
