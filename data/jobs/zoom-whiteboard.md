What you can expect

Zoom is seeking an experienced Lead Product Manager to drive the strategy and execution of our AI-native Zoom Whiteboard product. This role is central to shaping how teams collaborate, brainstorm, and visualize ideas across the Zoom platform. You will own the product vision and roadmap, lead features from concept to

launch, and use customer and market insights to deliver high-impact whiteboard experiences. You will collaborate closely with cross-functional partners, customers, and ecosystem partners to ensure solutions are successfully built, launched, and adopted.

About the Team

Zoom Whiteboard is a core part of Zoom’s productivity and collaboration suite. It provides a visual workspace where teams brainstorm, plan, and develop ideas. As Lead Product Manager, you will shape the future of visual collaboration. You will drive innovation in how users ideate and work together across the platform.

In this role, you will integrate whiteboard workflows with the Zoom platform, AI Companion, and key partnerships. You will scale adoption across priority customer segments. Powered by AI Companion, the team is transforming whiteboards into intelligent workspaces that capture ideas and turn them into action.

Responsibilities

    Scaling Zoom Whiteboard adoption among downmarket and online customers through go-to-market initiatives, product-led growth strategies, and strategic 3rd party integrations (e.g., Microsoft Teams,Slack, Google Workspace)

    Partnering with AI Companion and Workflow teams to build agentic workflow capabilities that transform whiteboards from static canvases into intelligent, automated workspaces

    Defining and execute the product vision, strategy, and roadmap for Zoom Whiteboard, with AI-driven experiences at the core

    Leading cross-functional teams (engineering, design, research, data, marketing) to deliver high-quality, customer-focused product releases

    Gathering and synthesize customer feedback, usage data, market research, and industry intelligence to inform product decisions

    Driving adoption, engagement, and retention through data-driven prioritization and experimentation

    Collaborating with marketing, sales, and customer success teams to ensure successful launches and clear product positioning

What we’re looking for

    Have experience driving product-led growth and scaling products in downmarket and online segments; demonstrated success with go-to-market strategies, channel partnerships, and viral adoption mechanics

    Have experience with 3rd party integration platforms and marketplace ecosystems; ability to identify and prioritize high-impact integrations that drive user acquisition and engagement

    Have understanding of agentic AI, workflow automation, and how AI can transform productivity tools into intelligent assistants that anticipate user needs and automate repetitive tasks

    Hold a Bachelor's in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field and experience working in agile development environments

    Have 7+ years in product management, including 3+ leading whiteboard, visual collaboration, or design tools products; proven in launching and scaling B2B SaaS used daily by professionals

    Have proven experience with digital whiteboard technologies, real-time collaboration platforms, and canvas-based products; AI- or automation-driven experience a nice to have

    Have solid knowledge of visual collaboration and design workflows, including brainstorming, diagramming, and creative ideation challenges in the enterprise productivity ecosystem

    Have ability to use data to drive product strategy and decisions

    Be excellent at aligning cross-functional teams, influencing stakeholders, and presenting to senior leadership
