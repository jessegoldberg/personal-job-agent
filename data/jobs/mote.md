
IT Support Specialist

Position: IT Support Specialist

Department: Information Systems

Reports to:  Director of Information Technology

Supervises: N/A

Introduction:

Are you an IT professional looking to take the next step in your career? Do you enjoy solving technical challenges, supporting users, and improving system reliability? If so, we want to hear from you.

Mote Marine is a non-profit research facility and major public attraction with multiple locations throughout the state. We’re seeking an Senior Information System Specialist with a minimum of 5 years of hands-on experience in Microsoft server environments. This role is ideal for someone who thrives in a fast-paced environment and wants to broaden their expertise across cloud services, identity management, and network operations.

Job Description:

    Provide day-to-day support (onsite and remote) for Microsoft Windows Server environments and endpoints
    Manage and maintain Active Directory, including user accounts, groups, and GPOs
    Assist with identity and access management in Microsoft Entra
    Support and troubleshoot non-standard technical and analytical equipment
    Schedule, execute and validate system backups using various tools.
    Review and interpret firewall logs and assist with basic security investigations
    Diagnose and resolve networking issues across LAN/WAN/Wi-Fi
    Respond to User support requests across a multi-location organization of over 300 users
    Collaborate on the creation of updated policies and procedures.
    Contribute with IT teammates on system updates, documentation, and process improvements
    Interface with end users to resolve support tickets efficiently and professionally

What We’re Looking For

    3–5 years of professional IT support experience
    Strong skills with desktops/laptops (iOS & MS)
    Experience in Microsoft Windows Server administration
    Working knowledge of Active Directory, Entra ID, and Azure
    Experience with firewall log analysis and network troubleshooting
    Familiarity with virtualization platforms (VMware/Hyper-V) a plus
    Curious, resourceful, and detail-oriented approach to problem-solving
    Strong communication skills and a customer-service mindset

Why You’ll Love Working With Us

    Competitive salary and excellent benefits
    Opportunities for ongoing training and professional development
    Supportive team environment with room to grow
    Exposure to modern cloud and security technologies
    Ongoing challenges as our environment continues to expand and evolve.
