## Role Description

The Lead Solutions Engineer will join ShipBob’s Revenue team, working with strategic prospects to understand their business needs and deliver tailored technical solutions.

This role partners with:
- Sales  
- Implementation  
- Customer Success  

The Lead SE acts as a trusted advisor, aligning merchant business objectives and technical requirements with ShipBob’s platform capabilities.

---

## What You'll Do

- **Architect Merchant Success**  
  Collaborate with strategic merchants to design solutions aligned with business goals and technical requirements  

- **Oversee Cross-Functional Execution**  
  Drive innovative, cross-functional solutions to improve sales conversion and accelerate deals  

- **Deliver Sales Excellence**  
  Support demos, warehouse tours, RFPs, vendor assessments, and technical evaluations  

- **Own Technical Discovery**  
  Analyze merchant environments to identify opportunities and guide scalable solution design  

- **Drive Strategic Rollouts**  
  Define technical vision and implementation roadmaps for successful product launches  

- **Stay Ahead of Industry Trends**  
  Maintain expertise in industry trends, ShipBob offerings, and competitive positioning  

- **Champion Product Innovation**  
  Align with product roadmap, evangelize features, and provide customer feedback  

- Perform additional duties as needed
