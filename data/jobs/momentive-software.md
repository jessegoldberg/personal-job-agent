# Solutions Engineer

## Overview

The Solutions Engineer (SE) is a key member of the Momentive Solutions Engineering organization, supporting a high-volume, fast-paced sales cycle (typically 90 days or less). This role partners closely with Account Executives to bring association domain knowledge together with product expertise to help membership-based organizations evaluate and adopt Momentive Software Association Management Software (AMS) solutions.

---

## General Function

The Solutions Engineer demonstrates how Momentive’s products support real-world association operations, including:

- Membership management  
- Engagement  
- Events  
- Committees  
- Reporting  

This role focuses on translating association workflows and challenges into compelling, value-driven demonstrations and conversations.

The SE acts as a trusted advisor during the evaluation process, connecting product capabilities to real-world association operations while contributing feedback to improve demos, messaging, and product direction.

---

## Duties & Responsibilities

- Prepare and deliver high-volume product demonstrations tailored to association-specific use cases:
  - Membership  
  - Renewals  
  - Events  
  - Engagement  
  - Reporting  
- Partner with Account Executives to support opportunities in a fast-moving sales cycle (90 days or less)  
- Assess prospect needs using experience with associations and common workflows  
- Translate association goals into clear product value (not overly technical explanations)  
- Serve as a subject-matter resource on association needs and constraints  
- Support RFP responses and follow-up questions with an association-first perspective  
- Participate in demo scheduling, preparation, and coordination  
- Provide product walkthroughs or light training during evaluations or pilots  
- Improve demo environments, scenarios, and demo videos based on real use cases  
- Capture customer/prospect feedback and relay insights to Product and Go-To-Market teams  
- Troubleshoot demo or environment-related issues  
- Assist with customer escalations when prior context is helpful
